SPARKLE Scheme

Student Program for Advancing Research, Knowledge, and Entrepreneurship

  • It involves a student team (at least 1 student from each year) responding to open challenges shared by faculty, industry, or alumni.
  • These challenges may be ambitious, with no fixed timelines for completion.
  • The SPARKLE initiative focuses on promoting discovery, collaboration skills, invention, and startups.
  • It provides opportunities for students to engage in long-term research problems and create patents or startups for BITS Pilani.
  • Maximum Funds to be allocated per Idea / Project / year : 2 Lakhs
  • During the first cycle, 85 projects have been selected
